Christopher R. Zanette

President, Insurance Broker

Chris started his insurance career in 1980 as an Aviation Insurance Underwriter in San Francisco, California. He then took a position as an Aviation Insurance Broker before leaving to start ZANETTE Aviation Insurance Service. Starting with local clients at the San Carlos Airport (SQL), ZANETTE has grown into a company with a proven reputation for excellence and clients across the greater United States. Chris learned to fly at 18 and now holds a Commercial Pilot Certificate with ASMEL and Instrument Ratings. Chris is a member of the EAA, AOPA, NBAA, and the HAI. He has been an elected member of the Board of Directors of the Aviation Insurance Association (AIA) as the Director of Agents & Brokers. He also serves on the Board of Directors for the local airport business association. In his spare time Chris enjoys Boating and day trips around the San Francisco Bay Area.

Carla Bottini Zanette

Insurance Broker (Team Leader)

Carla graduated from San Francisco State University with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Business Administration with a concentration in Marketing. Upon graduation she was recruited by The Gap Stores, Inc., as an accessory buyer working in their corporate office. Once her daughter and son turned school age, she transitioned as a Realtor working for Better Homes & Gardens Residential Real Estate. When Chris opened the business in 1989, Carla joined him as his business partner, co-owner, and Insurance Broker managing the many needs of the office. Today, Carla has over 25 years of experience as an aviation insurance specialist playing a very important role in the success of the company. In her free time Carla enjoys shopping and taking care of her two shelties, CoCo and Milo.

Christina Zanette Steach

Insurance Broker (Team Leader)

Christina graduated from San Diego State University with a Bachelor of Science degree in Public Administration and officially decided to join the family business in 2006. She received her Insurance Broker’s license soon after and now leads the ZANETTE marketing team. Growing up in an aviation family, Christina’s knowledge of aircraft is almost second nature and she holds a Private Pilot Certificate. In her free time she enjoys hiking with her husband, daughter, and their dog Charcoal.
